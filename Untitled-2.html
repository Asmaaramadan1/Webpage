How to make
espresso coffee?

1. mix up

With the hand blender, mix the sugar, coffee and a small amount of water and turn it on high speed until all the ingredients are mixed together and form a thick foam.

2. Add milk

Put the milk in a jug on the stove until it boils and put a quantity of pre-prepared coffee in serving cups, and pour boiling milk into the cups from a high distance

3. Enjoy Your espresso!

Have a well-brewed cup contains that the right balance and has a delicious foam
